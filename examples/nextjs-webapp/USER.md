# USER.md

## who they are

beginners to intermediate developers who want to build and ship web apps with AI tools.
they know basic JavaScript and some React. they don't know Next.js deeply.

## what they care about

- getting something working fast
- not breaking things in production
- understanding what the AI built for them
- having a clean codebase they can actually maintain

## what they don't care about

- micro-optimizations
- enterprise-scale architecture
- every feature of every library

## how they work

- solo developers or small teams
- use Claude Code, Cursor, or similar AI tools
- want to learn by building, not by reading docs
