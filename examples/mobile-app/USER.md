# USER.md

## who they are

mobile users who expect a fast, native-feeling experience.
they're on phones and tablets, often on mobile data, often in distracting environments.

## what they care about

- it works fast
- it works offline (at least partially)
- it doesn't drain their battery
- it looks good on their device

## what they don't care about

- the tech stack
- how data syncs
- error details (just tell them what to do)
