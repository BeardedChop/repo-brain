# USER.md

who is this project for? the AI needs to know so it makes decisions that serve the actual user, not some hypothetical power user.

## who they are

describe the person who will use this project.

- skill level: beginner? intermediate? expert?
- what they're trying to accomplish
- what they already know
- what they're stuck on

## what they care about

- what outcome do they want?
- what are they afraid of?
- what would make them trust this tool?

## what they don't care about

- what technical details would bore them?
- what features would they never use?
- what complexity should be hidden from them?

## how they work

- are they building alone or on a team?
- what tools do they already use?
- what's their typical workflow?

## accessibility

- language level: keep it simple
- assume no prior knowledge of X
- never condescend — beginners are smart, they're just new

## edge cases

are there user groups to specifically consider?
- non-native English speakers
- people on slow connections
- screen reader users
